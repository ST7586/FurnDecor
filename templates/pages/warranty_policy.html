Service Assurance / Warranty

Warranty Terms and Conditions

In case of any complaint during the warranty period or for out-of-warranty cases, customers can contact FurnDecor customer support for assistance.

  1. Customers must provide the original invoice to avail warranty services.
  2. Inspection will be carried out by authorized service personnel.
  3. Complaints will be resolved as per applicable warranty terms.
  4. Out-of-warranty complaints will be treated as paid service requests.
  5. Discontinued products will be repaired subject to spare availability.

General Warranty Terms

  1. FurnDecor Furniture carries up to three year manufacturers’ warranty from date of invoice, against manufacturing/material defects. Warranty for each product is specified based on product and material type is specified at point of sale. The warranty applies for repair, replacement of defective parts, wherever applicable.
  2. The invoice copy has to be produced for availing services under warranty, claim.
  3. FurnDecor will not be liable for any consequential damages and under no circumstances the liability will exceed the replacement or repair of defective parts as it may be.
  4. The warranty does not cover damages due to normal wear and tear during usage, improper fitments & installation by unauthorized technicians, inadequate maintenance, wrong handling, improper movements, pest infection, moisture and water seepage, exposed to direct sunlight, rusting due to improper maintenance, modifications made on standard product, usage beyond prescribed purpose and any negligence in usage.
  5. This warranty covers products used for domestic purpose only and not used for any commercial purpose or bulk usage.
  6. Any other conditions/liabilities which are not covered above will not be applicable under this warranty.
  7. Furndecor's decision on the nature of defects and applicability of warranty will be final.
  8. Any dispute will be under the jurisdiction of Mumbai courts only.
    9. Decision of the company regarding the nature of defects and applicability of warranty will be final.
  9. Warranty terms are subject to change without prior notice.
  10. No warranty on “AS IS” and Display products and few specified products: Customer can get paid service for such products subjected to availability of spare parts.
  11. Customer are advice to read use & care instruction carefully before using the products. This will help for better life of the products.  No warranty on selected products.
  12. Any brand products warranty will be provided by respective brands. Customer advice to refer the brand warranty policy.
